# Anthropic opens Seoul office and announces new partnerships across the Korean AI ecosystem

- Source: Anthropic News (2026.06.17, updated 06.18)
- URL: https://www.anthropic.com/news/seoul-office-partnerships-korean-ai-ecosystem

## Overview
Anthropic established its Seoul headquarters and announced multiple partnerships with
Korean enterprises, startups, and research institutions. The company also signed a
Memorandum of Understanding with South Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT focused on
AI safety advancement.

## Leadership
KiYoung Choi (최기영), 30 years of technology business experience in Korea, leads the
office as Representative Director.

## Government partnership
- MOU with Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT (과학기술정보통신부)
  - AI safety and cybersecurity collaboration
  - Model safety evaluation in Korean with the Korea AI Safety Institute (AI안전연구소)
  - Information exchange on AI-enabled cyber threats

## Enterprise adoption
- NAVER: Claude Code across entire engineering org (thousands of engineers)
- Nexon: Claude Code for live-service game development
- LG CNS: Rolling out Claude to thousands of employees for software development
- Hanwha Solutions: Deploying via AWS Bedrock with data-residency compliance
- Samsung SDS: Claude Code and Cowork across Samsung Electronics teams

## Startups & service providers
- Channel Corp: Channel Talk (230,000+ companies across Korea, Japan, US)
- WRTN, Law&Company: ongoing Claude adoption

## Academic research
- National AI Research Lab (NAIRL): KAIST, Korea University, Yonsei, POSTECH consortium;
  Claude access for up to 60 researchers on safety, evaluation, alignment, robustness

## Nonprofit
- Good Neighbors Korea (굿네이버스): program analysis and administrative efficiency

## Key stats
- Korea among top dozen countries globally for Claude.ai usage
- Claude Build Day: 100+ Korean founders and developers
- Claude Meetups since September 2025: hundreds of Korean developers

## Quotes
- KiYoung Choi: "What I see in Korea are teams who understand that innovation and safety
  are two sides of the same coin." (혁신과 안전은 동전의 양면)
